About Egoist

Egoist

EGOIST is a music duo. They were originally created for the anime Guilty Crown, but have continued releasing music separately since then.

Last Concert

EGOIST's final concert, "EGOIST LIVE 2023 Fleeting Ruby: The Crescendo," will take place on September 23rd in Osaka.

The DNA of Egoist

The band began self-publishing in 2007, born of the phenomenon that was Hatsune Miku and Vocaloid. By 2009, the group signed to Sony Music Entertainment Japan.

Beyond Guilty Crown

The band's first album after Guilty Crown was 2012's Extra Terrestrial Biological Entities, comprised of the band's themes from the TV series, in addition to a host of new songs.
